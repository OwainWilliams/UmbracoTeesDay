About

Every now and again the Umbraco Community come together and share photos of themselves on social media wearing their Umbraco merch.

This can be a tshirt, socks, hoodies, caps, a tshirt with an Umbraco sticker on it, anything. They share their photos using the hashtag #umbracoTees

The origin of #umbracoTees day was started Pete Duncanson was one of the first to announce an #umbracoTees day.

You can read Pete's reply to why he started #umbracoTees day on this blog post about #umbracoTees day

Where can I get Umbraco Merchandise from?

There are a number of ways to get your hands on Umbraco merch.

  • Attend an Umbraco conference such as Codegarden, UKFest, DuugFest, Umbraco Spark
  • Attend an Umbraco Meetup, sometimes they have merch to give away
  • Buy some from the Official Umbraco Swag store
  • Buy some from the Umbraco Community store
